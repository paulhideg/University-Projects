About Ice Cream Sandwich

Andy (born: February 8, 1996 [age 27]), better known online as Ice Cream Sandwich, is an American YouTuber and animator best known for his animated storytime videos and webcomics.

Andy lives in the United States with his dog, Rhea.
He has a day job, as YouTube is not his not his only source of income.
Andy went to college some time during the mid 2010's, and also studied abroad in France during that time.
Andy has two older sisters, Becca and Krista.
